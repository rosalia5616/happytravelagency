Welcome to Thailand

Bangkok

Grand Palace

The Grand Palace in Bangkok is a dazzling architectural masterpiece and the heart of Thailand's royal heritage. Built in 1782, it served as the official residence of the Kings of Siam. Visitors are captivated by the intricate golden spires, stunning murals, and the sacred Emerald Buddha housed within Wat Phra Kaew.

Chiang Mai

Doi Inthanon National Park

Known as "The Roof of Thailand," Doi Inthanon National Park is home to the country’s highest peak. It offers lush forests, stunning waterfalls, and peaceful pagodas. Perfect for nature lovers, the park also supports a diverse ecosystem of birds and flora, and offers unforgettable sunrise and sunset views.

Phuket

Phuket Island

Phuket is Thailand’s largest island, known for its soft sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lively atmosphere. Whether you're into snorkeling, beach bars, island hopping, or visiting temples like Wat Chalong, Phuket has something for everyone—from backpackers to luxury seekers.
