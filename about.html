About

1. Initial Consultation

The travel agent gathers the following information:

  • Destination and travel dates
  • Purpose of the trip
  • Budget and travel preferences

2. Drafting the Itinerary

A detailed day-by-day plan includes:

  • Transportation: Flights, transfers, local travel
  • Accommodation: Hotel details, check-in/out
  • Activities: Tours, sightseeing, leisure time
  • Dining: Restaurant suggestions, special experiences

3. Cost Breakdown

Includes all costs and what’s covered:

  • Airfare, hotel, tours
  • Insurance and service charges
  • What's not included (e.g., personal expenses)

4. Travel Information

Important information provided to the traveler:

  • Visa and passport requirements
  • Currency exchange tips
  • Vaccination and health advice

5. Booking and Confirmation

After approval, the agent books and shares confirmations, vouchers, and emergency contact details.

6. Ongoing Support

Travel agents provide support during the trip and resolve any issues that arise.

Sample Travel Plan

You’ll depart from New York on July 10th at 6:30 PM via Emirates Airlines and arrive in Paris the next morning. A private car will take you to Hotel Le Marais in central Paris for a 3-night stay.

Day 2: Half-day city tour including the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum.
Day 3: Full-day trip to Versailles.

Breakfast is included daily. A dinner cruise on the Seine River is scheduled for your final evening. Total cost: $2,400. Most expenses are covered except lunch and shopping.
